Welcome to TameLix!
This pack contains the Tame levels
from Oh No! More Lemmings, lovingly
rebuilt using Lix terrain and skills.

There are two sets:

Easy: Levels have 10 of each skill,
including the new Lix skills!

Hard: Challenges with tougher save
requirements, skills and time limits.
All can be done in the original
DOS game as well.

Thanks for playing!
